TSS GAMING Terms And Conditions

Growing faster every day, India is on track to become the face of E-sports especially in mobile games like Call Of Duty and Freefire. We at TSS Gaming help budding gamers by inviting them to explore and grow their gaming expertise by playing in tournaments organised by us.

TSS Gaming aims to provide young and talented players with a platform where they can display their epic gaming skills and develop them in a safe and friendly space.

At its core, it is a tournament hosting website that conducts tournaments where gamers of all skill levels and all around the globe compete against each other and win attractive prizes! TSS Gaming conducts these tournaments with popular mobile FPS (First Person Shooter) and TPP (Third Person Perspective) games like PUBGM KR, Counter-Strike, Call of Duty and Freefire.

TSS prides itself on organizing the highest quality and providing safely moderated tournaments. We at TSS, let each gamer explore their proficiency in different games and realise their full potential by playing and winning matches with attractive prize pools.
